Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

sunwatch is built to be as private as possible. This policy explains what we collect, how we store it, and what we do not do.

What we collect

  • The URL you want to monitor.
  • The HTTP status code you expect.
  • The webhook URL where we send up/down alerts.
  • A public wallet address used to activate paid monitors.

What we do not collect

  • No KYC or identity verification.
  • No email addresses or phone numbers.
  • No cookies or tracking pixels.
  • No third-party analytics.

Where data is stored

All data is stored locally on the sunwatch VPS in a SQLite database. We do not share data with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

How long we keep data

Monitor configuration and recent check results are kept as long as the monitor is active. If a monitor is removed, its data is deleted.

Questions or concerns? Open an issue on GitHub.
